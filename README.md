# Parking Now Privacy Policy

Parking Now (“the App”) values users’ privacy and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Parking Now mobile application and related services.

## 1. Personal Information We Collect

Parking Now is currently in a prototype and testing stage. At this stage, the App does not store personal information on a server or transmit personal information to external parties.

However, as the service develops, the following types of information may be collected:

- Vehicle license plate number
- Apartment complex name and resident verification information, such as building/unit information
- Parking usage records
- Exit request and notification history
- Device identification information and push notification tokens
- Error logs and app usage records generated while using the App

## 2. Purpose of Collection and Use

Personal information may be used for the following purposes:

- Verifying apartment resident status
- Providing vehicle registration and parking-related services
- Displaying real-time available parking areas
- Sending double-parking exit requests and related notifications
- Sending emergency alerts
- Improving app performance and service quality
- Preventing unauthorized use and maintaining service stability

## 3. Retention and Use Period

Parking Now will delete personal information without delay once the purpose of collection and use has been fulfilled.

However, if retention is required by applicable laws or regulations, the information may be stored for the legally required period.

## 4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Parking Now does not provide users’ personal information to third parties in principle.

However, information may be disclosed in the following cases:

- When the user has given prior consent
- When disclosure is required by law or requested through lawful procedures by investigative or administrative authorities
- When limited sharing with an apartment management office or related service operator is necessary to provide the service

## 5. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

Parking Now does not currently outsource the processing of personal information to external service providers.

If external services such as servers, notifications, authentication, or payment systems are introduced in the future, the relevant service providers and processing details will be updated in this Privacy Policy.

## 6. User Rights

Users may request the following regarding their personal information at any time:

- Access to personal information
- Correction of personal information
- Deletion of personal information
- Suspension of personal information processing
- Withdrawal from the service

Requests regarding personal information may be submitted through the contact information below.

## 7. Measures to Protect Personal Information

Parking Now may take the following measures to protect personal information:

- Restricting access to personal information
- Encrypting data
- Minimizing unnecessary collection of personal information
- Reviewing security vulnerabilities
- Updating app and server security measures

## 8. Protection of Children’s Personal Information

Parking Now is not primarily intended for children under the age of 14.

If Parking Now becomes aware that personal information of a child under the age of 14 has been collected, such information will be deleted without delay.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to changes in service features, laws, regulations, or operational policies.

Any changes will be announced through the App or this page.
